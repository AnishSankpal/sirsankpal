My Coding Journey

The journey leading up to this point

  • June 2020

    The Beginning

    I began my coding journey by enrolling in the Scratch course at "Sanketana School of Code". Scratch was not the most complex or difficult coding language to learn, but it helped me build a strong foundation for coding and taught me how to think differently in the context of coding projects.

  • September 2021 - March 2023

    My Break from Coding

    I took a long break from coding to focus on my studies. My examinations were approaching, and I believed that focusing on them was more important. I would occasionally create a small game on Scratch, but without any assistance, even minor obstacles would often leave me feeling quite unmotivated.

  • March 2023

    My Return to Coding

    After my examinations were over, I felt motivated to resume coding; however, I wanted to learn a slightly more complex programming language. This is why I chose to learn Python. Learning Python was the most enjoyable coding experience I've ever had, and I picked it up quite quickly. I would genuinely love to continue working with this language in the future.

  • July 2023

    The Start of a Different Language

    After completing my Python course, I wasn't ready to stop coding just yet. That's why I decided to learn HTML and CSS. I've always been a creative person, and the chance to create my own websites with my artistic skills was an opportunity I couldn't resist!
